Dimelo Papi

Hello friend! It’s nice to meet you! My name is Joel (Jo-el). I am very excited to have you visiting my website. I am a photographer based in Reading, Pennsylvania. I picked up my first DSLR in 2017. What started as a hobby doing some street and landscape photography, quickly became my passion. I always loved photography but was always afraid to try it as I am an introvert who didn’t speak to a lot of people. One day some friends asked me to take their photos, from that moment on I fell in love with portraiture photography. I love capturing special moments and bringing different ideas to life.
